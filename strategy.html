My Trading Strategy

Clear rules. Consistent results. This is the approach I use to win in the markets.

Core Principles

  • Price Action: I trade using pure price action, focusing on key support and resistance levels, breakouts, and market structure.
  • Risk Management: Every trade is planned with strict risk, never risking more than 1% of my capital per trade.
  • Discipline & Patience: No revenge trading, no overtrading. Only the best setups, always waiting for confirmation.
  • Review & Improvement: I journal every trade and analyze both wins and losses to keep improving.

My Setup Checklist

  1. Identify clear trend or range in a high timeframe
  2. Mark key levels and wait for price reaction
  3. Wait for confirmation (candle close, wick rejection, etc.)
  4. Set stop loss and take profit before entering
  5. Execute and follow the plan—never move stop loss

Favorite Tools

TradingView charts, session volume profile, clean candlesticks, and alert levels. No indicators—just market structure and price.
